Nature Gallery

This gallery presents nine simple landscape illustrations inspired by sunrise light, lakes, forests, coasts, mountain views, and open steppe scenes. The goal is to keep the page readable and well structured while still making the layout feel different on phones, tablets, and large screens.

The design uses a mobile-first grid, responds to dark mode preferences, and turns off smooth scrolling for people who prefer reduced motion. Every image includes descriptive alternative text so the gallery is easier to understand for screen readers and easier to review during accessibility checks.
